Our Vision

Established 2025

Commitment to Empowerment

Founded in May 2025 under the vision of Mr. Muhammad Haroon Mahmood, a respected industrialist and philanthropist, the Haroon Mahmood Welfare Trust is dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities across Pakistan through privately mobilized resources. Since its inception, We have been actively working in livelihood support, healthcare, education, and sustainable community development.

Our Vision

  • A dignified and self‑reliant society for vulnerable families.
  • Reliable access to healthcare, education, and livelihood support.
  • Inclusive development that empowers individuals to become self‑sustaining.
  • Long‑term social impact through community‑focused support systems.

Our Mission

  • Financial & livelihood support for vulnerable families.
  • Access to education and vocational training for youth and women.
  • Healthcare assistance for the poor and medically needy.
  • Community welfare aligned with corporate social responsibility (CSR) values.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Honesty and strong moral principles in all our dealings.
  • Transparency: Open and clear communication in all operations.
  • Community First: Prioritizing the needs and well-being of the communities we serve.
  • Sustainability: Ensuring long-term positive impact through resilient programs.

Governance & Financial Integrity

HM Welfare Trust operates with strong governance, transparency, and Shariah compliance. All financial transactions are fully documented and audited.

Disbursements follow a three-step review: Verification, Approval, and Audit.

Shariah Compliant | Audited | Documented | EAD Compliant
