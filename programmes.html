Our Impact & initiatives

Key Achievements

Monthly stipends ensuring income stability for widows & families.

Scholarships and vocational training for youth under Ilm-O-Hunar.

Medical treatment and diagnostics funded through Negehban-E-Sehat.

Business startup support and livelihood through Sahara Programme.

Assistance to community and religious institutions via Sufra-E-Khair.

100% traceable disbursements under digital monitoring & Shariah oversight.

How We Work

Verification

Thorough field verification of every beneficiary to ensure aid reaches the most deserving.

Approval

Multi-step review process by management and the Shariah Committee for accountability.

Direct Impact

Funds are disbursed directly through formal banking channels for 100% transparency.

Program Metrics & Scope

Monthly Kifala

Direct cash assistance ensuring dignity and stability for verified families in need.

Objectives:

  • Provide regular, traceable financial support.
  • Stabilize household income for vulnerable families.

Beneficiaries:

  • Widows, orphans, and low-income families.
  • Verified cases screened by field teams.
80 Verified Beneficiaries

Negehban-E-Sehat

Specialized medical intervention and diagnostic support for critical cases.

Objectives:

  • Enable access to diagnostics, treatment, and surgeries.
  • Reduce preventable morbidity through timely support.

Beneficiaries:

  • Medically needy patients unable to afford care.
  • Elderly, widows, and rural communities.
3 Major Cases Funded

Ilm-O-Hunar

Empowering youth through academic scholarships and vocational training.

Objectives:

  • Reduce dropout rates and promote literacy.
  • Build employable skills via vocational training.

Beneficiaries:

  • Orphaned children and low-income students.
  • Youth seeking skill development.
25 Active Students

Sahara Program

Localized livelihood support and micro-business grants in the Haripur region.

Objectives:

  • Kickstart or grow small, sustainable businesses.
  • Strengthen household resilience and income.

Beneficiaries:

  • Low-income families and micro-entrepreneurs.
  • Verified cases in Haripur and nearby areas.
25 Small Businesses

Sufra-E-Khair

Food & ration distribution to combat hunger in neglected districts.

Objectives:

  • Eliminate hunger and provide nutritional security.
  • Support community kitchens and relief drives.

Beneficiaries:

  • Families living below the poverty line.
  • Community and religious institutions serving the needy.
80+ Families Supported
