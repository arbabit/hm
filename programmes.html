Our Impact & initiatives

Dedicated programs designed to foster sustainable growth, health, and education across underprivileged communities.

Key Achievements

Monthly stipends ensuring income stability for widows & families.

Scholarships and vocational training for youth under Ilm-O-Hunar.

Medical treatment and diagnostics funded through Negehban-E-Sehat.

Business startup support and livelihood through Sahara Programme.

Assistance to community and religious institutions via Sufra-E-Khair.

100% traceable disbursements under digital monitoring & Shariah oversight.

Our Mission
To create sustainable impact through programs that promote:
  • Financial & livelihood support for vulnerable families.
  • Access to education and vocational training for youth and women.
  • Healthcare assistance for the poor and medically needy.
  • Community welfare aligned with corporate social responsibility (CSR) values.

Education Support Programme

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and vocational training to children from low-income families.

  • Objectives: Reduce dropout rates and promote literacy.
  • Beneficiaries: Orphaned children and low-income students.

Healthcare & Medical Aid

Offering free medical checkups, essential medicines, and emergency surgery funds for those who cannot afford treatment.

  • Objectives: Accessible primary healthcare for all.
  • Beneficiaries: Elderly, widows, and rural communities.

Food & Ration Distribution

Monthly food hampers and clean water initiatives to combat malnutrition in neglected districts.

  • Objectives: Eliminate hunger and provide nutritional security.
  • Beneficiaries: Families living below the poverty line.
